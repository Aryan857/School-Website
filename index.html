    About

    Bombay Scottish School is a Christian School that attempts to impart Christian values to children. Vision: To envisage our students abide by the moral values imparted to them, have a true civic sense, exhibit compassion towards their fellow beings, and lead an honest and just life. Mission: We enkindle the spirit of patience, humility, hard work and perseverance in our students to be ideal citizens. The academic year (which begins in June and ends in April) comprises two terms: June-October, November-April. The courses of studies extend from the Pre-School stage (KG) to Standard X at the end of which pupils appear for the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education Examination.
